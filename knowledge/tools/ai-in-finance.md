# AI in Finance

Course material for the class on AI in Finance at NYU Stern, created and taught by Professor Arpit Gupta.

## Overview

This repository contains syllabus, lecture notes, and materials for an open course on applying AI to finance. The course explores foundational concepts including Amdahl's Law, Jevons' Paradox, and lessons from why the finance industry was historically slow to adopt AI insights.

## Resources

- **Syllabus:** Available in Dropbox link included in README
- **Lecture Notes:** Organized by topic in repository directories
- **Weekly Summaries:** Published on Substack at https://arpitrage.substack.com/p/1-three-rules-for-ai-in-finance

## Topics Covered

- Amdahl's Law applications in finance
- Jevons' Paradox and productivity
- Historical patterns in finance AI adoption
- Three Rules for AI in Finance
