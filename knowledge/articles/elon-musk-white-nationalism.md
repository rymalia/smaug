# Elon Musk Just Endorsed Blatant White Nationalism (And the Silence is Deafening)

**Source:** Religion Dispatches
**Author:** Ben Lorber
**Published:** Friday, January 9, 2026

## Summary

Analysis examining Elon Musk's retweet of the phrase "white solidarity is the only way to survive" and the surprisingly minimal media coverage of this endorsement of white nationalist rhetoric by the world's richest person.

## Key Points

- Elon Musk retweeted content promoting white nationalism
- The incident received minimal mainstream media coverage despite its significance
- The piece argues that our "numb overwhelmed media environment" enabled this to slip by with little public attention
- This represents a significant moment where a billionaire with enormous platform influence openly endorsed extremist ideology

## Significance

This article examines the troubling reality that a figure with Musk's influence and reach can publicly endorse white nationalist content with relatively little pushback or coverage from major news outlets, raising concerns about both the normalization of extremism and the state of media attention in the current moment.
