# How Trump Turned a Foreign Country Into a Piggybank

**Author:** Jonathan V. Last
**Publication:** The Bulwark

## Summary

An examination of Trump's foreign policy approach to Venezuela, focusing on how the administration garnished Venezuelan oil money and directed it to Qatar. The article explores Marco Rubio's role in using Trump's policies to target Cuba and the broader implications of using a foreign country as a financial tool for US policy goals.

## Why This Matters

This article provides critical insight into the Trump administration's unconventional approach to foreign policy, particularly regarding Venezuela and Cuba. It highlights the intersection of economic sanctions, political maneuvering, and international finance in shaping US-Latin American relations.

## Key Takeaways

- The US garnished Venezuelan oil money under Trump's administration
- These funds were directed to a bank account in Qatar
- Marco Rubio played a significant role in shaping these policies to affect Cuba
- The approach represents an unusual use of a foreign country's assets for US policy objectives
