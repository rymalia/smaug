# Meta Buried Causal Evidence of Social Media Harm

Reuters report on Meta's alleged suppression of internal research into the mental health effects of Facebook.

## Key Finding
Meta shut down internal research into the mental health effects of Facebook after finding causal evidence that its products harmed users' mental health.

## Significance
- Raises questions about corporate transparency in research
- Highlights potential conflicts of interest in internal research
- Relevant to broader social media regulation and ethics discussions
